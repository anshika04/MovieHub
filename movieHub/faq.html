FAQs


1. What different roles are present in MovieHub Website?

1. Customer - To access and purchase all movies, user should Login or Signup.

2. Guest - Guest User can access only free movies and have multiple restrictions on accesss.

3. Admin - Admin can Add, Delete, Update Movies and Customer Subscriptions.Admin can modify and monitor customer orders based on google graphs. Admin can conduct Data Analysis.

2. Who can access free movies?
- Guest and Registered Customer can access free movies for a span of 30 minutes only.

3. Can Guest watch other than free movies?
- No, Guest is required to register for this accessablity.

4. What different genres of movies available?
- Action, Animated, Classic, Comedy and Horror.

5. How can you find a movie?
1. You can find movies based on categories present in left navigation bar.
2. You can search on search bar present at top.

6. Until when can a user watch subscribed movies?
- Subscription lasts for 2 weeks and is available for renewal on expiry.

7. What payment options available?
- Credit Cards only.

8. Where can a user drop in the review?
- User can write a review by clicking Write Review button present at bottom of each available movies.

9. Where can a User view all the reviews for a movie?
- User can view reviews by clicking View Review button present at bottom of each available movies.

10. Can a Guest write a review for paid movies?
- No, Guest cannot access paid movies and so is not allowed to write or view a review.

11. What does Trending option do?
- Trending lets you view the ongoing top movies list based on criterian.

12. What movies are coming soon to MovieHub?
- You can view the coming soon movies in the topmost active slider.
